Daniel Billard

As a real estate broker, Daniel sees each transaction as a unique situation and strives to find the best result for the client. No matter whether you're buying your first home in Canada or downsizing from your current property, Daniel's no pressure approach will ensure you get unbiased, professional advice throughout the transaction.
